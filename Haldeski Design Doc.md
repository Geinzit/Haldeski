## Attribute

### Hatred

One of the most primal emotions. The fuel for destruction and persecution. And no creature knows more about directing and wielding them than humans.

### Avarice

A primal emotion twisted from the feeling of wanting. A great driver of evolution and progression. However, human's want only becomes greater when satisfied.

### Lust

A primal emotion twisted from the need for companionship. An emptiness that requires the satisfaction of flesh to temporary quench, never fully, the ultimate irony.
